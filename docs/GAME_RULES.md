# Game Rules and Mechanics

## Game Overview

Based on the official manual, Skannerz Racerz features a comprehensive system with multiple difficulty levels, racing styles, and vehicle progression mechanics.

## Game Statistics

### Vehicles
- **120** hot rods and trucks
    - **63** Obtainable via races [list](./CARS_AND_RACERS.md#racer-car-list)

### Car Parts
- **64** car parts available [list](./ITEMS.md)

### Competitors
- **21** [total competitors](./CARS_AND_RACERS.md#competitors)
  - **9** Rookie level
  - **6** Amateur level
  - **6** Pro level

### Racing System
- **3** race types (Off-Road, Drag, Street)
- **3** difficulty levels (Rookie, Amateur, Pro)

## Vehicle Attributes

Every vehicle has four performance attributes:
- Speed (SP)
- Acceleration (AC)
- Suspension (SU)
- Traction (TR) 

## Race Types and Mechanics

### Off-Road Racing
Represented by a mountain icon. 

**Primary Attribute**: Suspension (SU)  
**Secondary Attribute**: Acceleration (AC)

### Drag Racing
Represented by a straight street icon. 

**Primary Attribute**: Acceleration (AC)  
**Secondary Attribute**: Speed (SP)

### Street Racing
Represented by an S-shaped street icon 

**Primary Attribute**: Speed (SP)  
**Secondary Attribute**: Suspension (SU)
